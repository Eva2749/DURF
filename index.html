The Paramount

Late one night, the sound of music from dance hall came floating through the air from afar.

A sharp, thin female voice was singing: "Roses, roses, blooming everywhere!"

On such a cruel night, a night so big and so broken,

It was impossible to imagine that any roses would be blooming."

- "On Music", Eileen Chang (1944)

Papers

Shixiang and I need 2000 to get one.

It is not a small number, but we are confident.

- "1000 Sweet Papers", Ning Tie

Shanghainese

My relatives from the countryside were often overwhelmed with cars and foreign buildings.

They slept on the floor, woke up at dawn, hearing the cock crow, and sighed:

"This is Shanghainese".

- "Shanghai Fu", Xin Mu
